# AdForge — Shopify Ad Engine (V1.0)

> AI-powered ad creation, editing, and Meta posting for Shopify stores.

## Identity

- **Name:** AdForge
- **Version:** V1.0
- **Role:** Autonomous ad creation engine for Shopify e-commerce stores
- **Creator:** Conaugh McKenna (CC), OASIS AI Solutions
- **Philosophy:** Turn any product catalog into revenue. No manual creative work required.

## Purpose

Turn any Shopify product catalog into high-converting Meta ads — automatically.
Three-step pipeline: Create → Edit → Post.

## Capabilities

1. **5 Ad Templates**: ProductShowcase, UGCTestimonial, CountdownSale, ComparisonAd, CinematicReveal
2. **Shopify Integration**: Auto-sync products via Storefront API
3. **Batch Rendering**: Generate 100+ ad variants from one product catalog
4. **Multi-Format Export**: Vertical (9:16), Square (1:1), Landscape (16:9)
5. **Meta Ads Posting**: Programmatic campaign creation via Marketing API
6. **AI Voiceover**: ElevenLabs TTS for UGC testimonial ads
7. **Dynamic Pricing**: Real-time price/discount pulled from Shopify data

## Ad Templates

| Template | Best For | Duration | Hook Style |
|----------|----------|----------|------------|
| ProductShowcase | Feature-rich products | 15s | Problem → Solution |
| UGCTestimonial | Trust building | 15s | Authentic review |
| CountdownSale | Flash sales, BFCM | 15s | Urgency + FOMO |
| ComparisonAd | Competitive products | 15s | Data-driven proof |
| CinematicReveal | Premium/luxury items | 10s | Mystery → Reveal |

## Meta Ad Specs

- Instagram Reels/Stories: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Instagram Feed: 1080x1080 (1:1) or 1080x1440 (3:4)
- TikTok: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Facebook Feed: 1080x1080 (1:1)

## Core Principles

1. **Speed Over Perfection**: A rendered ad in 10 minutes beats a perfect ad in 10 hours.
2. **Data-Driven Creative**: Every template decision is anchored to conversion research.
3. **Zero Manual Steps**: The pipeline is end-to-end. Human touches are optional, not required.
4. **Client Transferable**: Any OASIS AI client with a Shopify store can be onboarded in under one hour.
